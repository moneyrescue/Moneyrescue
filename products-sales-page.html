Why Most People Stay
Stuck in Debt for Years

It's not a lack of willpower. It's a lack of a clear, personalized system. Generic advice fails because it doesn't account for your specific debts, income, and psychology.

"I've tried budgeting before and it never sticks."
Most budgets fail because they're built around restriction, not clarity. Our system shows you exactly where every dollar goes — removing the guesswork that causes people to give up.
"I don't know which debt to pay off first."
Without a prioritized order, you're throwing money randomly at debt and barely making progress. Our checklist and calculator give you a numbered attack order in minutes.
"I keep paying minimums and the balance never drops."
At 22% APR, minimums barely cover interest. Our workbook shows you exactly how much interest you're losing monthly and how to stop it.
"I paid off a card and just ran it back up again."
This is the most common setback — and it's a system problem, not a character flaw. Our approach builds habits that prevent backsliding permanently.
