01 Information We Collect

When you visit moneyrescue.github.io ("the Site"), we may collect the following types of information:

Information You Provide

  • Email address, if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter or request a free resource
  • Any information you submit through contact forms

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, time on page, and referring URLs
  • Clicks on advertisements displayed on the Site

This information is collected through cookies and third-party analytics tools described below.

02 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use cookies to improve your experience and serve relevant advertisements. The Site uses the following types:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the site to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies — Used to understand how visitors interact with the site (via Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies — Used to serve personalized ads based on your interests (via Google AdSense)

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality and ad personalization.

How to manage cookies in your browser

Visit your browser's Help section and search "manage cookies" — or go directly to: Chrome Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies. Similar options exist in Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

03 Google AdSense

This Site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is operated by Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA.

Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

  • Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve interest-based ads
  • Third-party vendors may also use cookies to serve ads on this site
  • Ad serving is subject to Google's advertising policies

Opt out of personalized ads: You can opt out of Google's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data from sites that use its services, visit: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

04 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to arriving here.

  • We use this data only to improve the Site's content and user experience
  • Google Analytics data is anonymized where possible
  • We do not combine Analytics data with personally identifiable information

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Affiliate Disclosure

Data collected through this Site is used for the following purposes:

07 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you

  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from emails at any time via the unsubscribe link
  • Data portability — Request your data in a portable format

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA. EU/UK residents have rights under GDPR. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.
