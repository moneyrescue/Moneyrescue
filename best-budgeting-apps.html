How to Choose the Right Budgeting App

The best budgeting app is the one you'll actually open every day. Features matter less than habit. That said, a few questions narrow it down quickly:

  • Want the best free option? Empower gives you budgeting, debt tracking, and net worth all in one place at zero cost
  • Are you on iPhone only? Copilot has the best iOS experience hands down
  • Do you budget as a couple? Monarch Money has the best shared budgeting features
  • Are you a complete beginner? Simplifi is the gentlest on-ramp at just $3.99/month
  • Want completely free with credit monitoring? NerdWallet covers both at no cost
Our honest take: Start with Empower — it's free, connects to everything, and gives you a complete financial picture immediately. If you want more structured zero-based budgeting, Monarch Money at $9.99/month is the best paid upgrade and well worth it for serious debt payoff tracking.

Budgeting Tips
Start smallTrack just one spending category for a week before going all-in. Building the habit matters more than perfection.
Connect all accountsApps only work if they can see your full picture — link every card and account on day one.
Weekly check-ins beat dailySpending 15 minutes every Sunday reviewing the week is more sustainable than daily micro-tracking.
Don't quit after a bad monthEvery budgeter blows their budget sometimes. The app is a tool, not a report card.
